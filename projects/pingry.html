Deep Mapping Pottersville

Welcome to Deep Mapping Pottersville, a project created by students at the Pingry School. Making a deep map is a way to know a place and the many layered understandings present there in a holistic, expansive way. From the flora and fauna, weather and geology, the native people, the colonists, and the current residents, to the agricultural and mechanical, all aspects, contradictions, and memories are valid and valuable in constructing a deep map of a place.

This portion of our project is a digital mapping tool that allows you to record and upload a variety of media to build a deep understanding of Pottersville, NJ. The map is a work in progress to which you are invited to contribute your own places and participate in building a deep map of Pottersville.

The points on this map record nature, poetry, science, art, and history. They have been placed here or illuminated by people young, old, and in between who have contributed their knowledge, memories, and insights.

Move the map around, click the colored points, and discover Pottersville.
