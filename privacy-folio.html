FOLIO Privacy Statement

FOLIO is fully committed to compliance with the requirements of Data Protection as amended by the General Data Protection Requirements (GDPR).

FOLIO follows procedures which aim to ensure that all employees, volunteers, trustees and others who have access to any personal data held by or on behalf of FOLIO are fully aware of and abide by their duties under the legislation.

Deep Map Project
